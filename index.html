A Novel Heat Exchanger flow which can be applied to any existing Heat Exchanger to reduce its cost by over 12%. The flow path has a maximum effectiveness equal to counterflow Heat Exchangers, but it is either more compact for equal effectiveness, or more effective for equal Heat Exchanger size.

Analytical and Computational Assessment of Performances of Flat and Toroidal Drone Propellers

The results showed that the toroidal propellers significantly outperformed the flat propellers in lift, generating between 6 - 77% more thrust across all blade configurations, while significantly trailing it in efficiency, with the flat propellers generating between 45 - 64% more thrust per watt across all blade configurations.
